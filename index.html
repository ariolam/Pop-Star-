Biography

80s

His great success began when the young artist from Rome reached the finals of the Voci Nuove di Castrocaro competition with "Rock 80", the song which got him his first record contract with the DDD label.

90s

The official consecration of Eros Ramazzotti as an international artist arrived in April 1990, with the release of his fifth album,"In ogni senso", in 15 counries.

00s

Two more successful albums followed: "Calma apparente", containing his famous duet with Anastasia, "I Belong To You", followed by a European tour, and "e",with 14 of hits in original remastered versions and 4 previously unreleased tracks.
